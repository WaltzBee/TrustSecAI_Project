TrustSec AI Lab

Mission

Develop robust and resilient artificial intelligence (AI) models to detect, withstand, and mitigate emerging AI-driven cyber threats. The TrustSec AI Lab advances research at the intersection of AI and cybersecurity, building AI-driven solutions that enhance the security, robustness, and trustworthiness of interconnected digital systems.

Protecting the next generation of AI

The TrustSec AI Lab focuses on securing, governing, and building trust in AI systems. By examining risk, policy, transparency, and accountability, we develop frameworks that ensures AI technologies are safe, ethical, and aligned with human values. We focus on:

  • Building resilient and trustworthy AI systems
  • Identifying vulnerabilities and adversarial threats
  • Securing data and network integrity
  • Defending large lagnuage models and Machine Learning systems
  • Training students in applied AI security

Foundation Work

We advance core research in machine learning (ML) and deep learning (DL) to identify vulnerabilities and build defenses against adversarial AI systems, with focus areas including:

  • Network Security- Threat detection across complex, distributed environments.
  • Data leakage - Preventing sensitive information exposure and model inversion.
  • Adversarial AI - Attacks, robustness analysis, and certified defenses.
  • Large Language Models (LLMs) - Risks, attacks, and secure deployment.

Why It Matters

AI powers decision-making across sectors by processing vast, high-velocity data streams. The TrustSec AI Lab ensures these systems remain reliable, robust, and worthy of trust, safeguarding societal impact and mission-critical operations.

Potential Impact on Education

  • Helping students engage with real-world problems to help develop practical skills and different defensive strategies.
  • Students develop an understanding of ethical responsibility about Artificial Intelligence.
  • Empowering students to advocate for responsible policies and regulations around AI, leading to future framworks for ethical AI use.
